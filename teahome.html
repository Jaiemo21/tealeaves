TEA LEAVES DIESEASE HOME

The leaves pictures can be used for the prediction of various tea leaves Diseases which causes huge lose in agricultural system every year. The proposed system -Tea leaves image processing system helps to the analysis of leaves and thereby help us in predicting various leaves diseases. The input to the proposed system is the Tea leaves images. The leaves portion is segmented and leaves cour, shape and texture features are extracted and taken together to form a feature vector and then analysis of leaves are done wich is used for the diagnosis of varous tea leaves diseases. Tis proposed system will help the farmers in the early diagnosis of diseases.
